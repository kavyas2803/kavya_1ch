From Cost Burden to Cloud Brilliance: Shearwater's Analytics Reinvention

1CloudHub empowers Shearwater Health with cloud-native analytics powered by AWS and Generative AI.

Shearwater Health, a global leader in Clinical Process Outsourcing® (CPO®), faced challenges in scaling its intelligence operations. High BI license costs, delayed insights, and limited adoption hindered decision-making. 1CloudHub stepped in, migrating from legacy BI to AWS QuickSight and automating ETL processes with AWS Glue (PySpark, PyAthena). They further integrated Generative AI for enhanced, smart data storytelling.

This transformation resulted in 70% faster reporting, 70% cost savings, and empowered 1,000 clinicians to make faster and more accurate healthcare decisions.

Real-Time Analytics in Action

The implementation of AWS QuickSight has revolutionized how Shearwater Health processes and visualizes their clinical data. The dashboards below demonstrate the power of cloud-native analytics, showcasing real-time insights into patient outcomes, operational efficiency, and resource utilization across multiple healthcare facilities.

These interactive dashboards enable healthcare professionals to identify trends, monitor key performance indicators, and make data-driven decisions that directly impact patient care quality. The integration of Generative AI further enhances the user experience by providing contextual insights and automated data storytelling.
